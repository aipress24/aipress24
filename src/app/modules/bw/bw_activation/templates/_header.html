{# `hx-boost` is INTENTIONALLY off on the BW-tunnel header logo. When the user clicks « back to platform » (i.e. the logo), they leave the BW tunnel and land on the wire / news feed. That feed renders article cards via an Alpine-driven `Carousel` component whose slides start with `style="display: none"` — Alpine flips them to visible on `x-init`. With `hx-boost` on, htmx swaps the body without re-running Alpine init → carousels stay hidden → user sees the news page with no photos until they hit refresh. Ref: bug #0109 part 1. The « retour vers la plateforme » buttons in the body (`_back_to_platform.html`) similarly avoid `hx-boost` for the same reason. #}
